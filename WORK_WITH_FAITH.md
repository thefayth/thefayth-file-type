## Best-Fit Engagements

### Protected File / Provenance Consulting

For people who need better proof, authorship, original-file, and selective disclosure workflows.

Outcome: practical provenance strategy, local receipt structure, public/private boundary, and a protected-file workflow plan.

### GitHub / Public Surface Packaging

For private tools that need a credible public GitHub surface without publishing the private engine.

Outcome: public-safe docs, ownership posture, security language, launch receipt, and commercial CTA alignment.

### Local AI Workflow Setup

For builders who want AI help without losing control of files, receipts, approvals, or production boundaries.

Outcome: workflow map, task packet templates, receipt paths, and safe automation rules.

### AI Brand System Audit

For brands whose public copy, visuals, docs, and product story need clearer trust and sharper positioning.

Outcome: brand-fit review, visual language notes, rejected-pattern list, and next-batch creative direction.

### AI Website Visual Cleanup

For sites with weak visuals, repeated assets, broken images, generic AI art, inconsistent crops, or low public trust.

Outcome: route-by-route visual recommendations and publish-ready handoff.

## How To Start

Send a short note through [FaithCheltenham.com/contact](https://faithcheltenham.com/contact/) with:

- the public URL, repo, website, or workflow you want reviewed
- the outcome you want the work to make possible
- the timeline or launch window
- whether you need strategy, implementation, review-only, or a demo

Do not send passwords, app passwords, API keys, signed URLs, private records, or production credentials in the first message.
